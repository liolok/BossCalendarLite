Calculate and remember when bosses respawn, with click-announcing and auto-reminding.

By default, press and hold "V" to view the calendar.

[h2]Mod Settings[/h2]
[list]
    [*]Key to open the calendar
    [*]Unit of respawn time: days in game or time in reality
    [*]Style of game day: absolute or countdown
    [*]Color of respawn reminder
    [*]Duration of respawn reminder
[/list]
